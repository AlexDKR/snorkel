  • NPN Silicon Epitaxial Planar Transistors

  • These transistors are subdivided into three groups A, B, and C according to their current gain.

    The type BC546 is available in groups A and B, however, the types BC547 and BC548 can be supplied in all three groups. The BC549 is a low-noise type and available in groups B and C. As complementary types the PNP transistors BC556...BC559 are recommended.

  • On special request, these transistors are also manufactured in the pin configuration TO-18.
